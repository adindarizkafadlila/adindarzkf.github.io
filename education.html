2006 - 2012 SD Plus Bina 45 Pontianak

SD Plus Bina Empat Lima, which was established from 2001 to 2005, does not yet have a learning building and is still living in Bina Empat Lima Kindergarten. Starting in 2005, the SD Plus Bina Empat Lima learning building was built with the laying of the first stone by the head of the foundation. The school building was completed in 2007 with 6 classes, 1 principal's office and 1 teacher's office. In 2010 the construction of the library and UKS room was continued. Then in 2015, the building and school environment were renovated, such as courtyards, ablution places, toilets, gardening, and school fences.

2012 - 2015 SMP Negeri 11 Pontianak

SMP Negeri 11 Pontianak is a public junior high school that serves the teaching of junior high school education in Pontianak City. The lessons provided include all compulsory subjects according to the applicable curriculum. SMP Negeri 11 Pontianak has a teaching staff of teachers who are competent in their field of study so that they are of high quality and become one of the best in Pontianak City. There are also various school facilities such as comfortable classrooms, libraries, sports fields, canteens and others.

2019 - Now Politeknik Negeri Pontianak

Pontianak State Polytechnic (POLNEP) is a professional pathway higher education system that emphasizes the mastery and development of Science and Technology to support the industrialization era. In general, the Polytechnic education system has the following characteristics: 1. Higher education with competency-based curriculum. 2. Professional education that emphasizes the formation of competencies to handle technology work according to standard practice. 3. Teaching theory and practice are held to strengthen each other's reasoning abilities and skills in dealing with practical problems. Theory teaching emphasizes linking basic concepts with real cases directly through practical solving methods, while practical teaching emphasizes the ability to integrate theory with handling real processes to produce finished products.
